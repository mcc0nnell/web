Principles
Usage rules
Do
  • Use the authentic NAD logo from the ticker in shell rails, brand strips, and sanctioned badges.
  • Let gold signal importance and keep navy as the stage.
  • Prefer bands, rails, and separators over repetitive dashboard boxes.
  • Save animation for moments: category reveal, countdown urgency, answer reveal, champion state.
  • Keep the family coherent across operator, audience, signage, ticker, and lower thirds.
Avoid
  • No esports neon or random cyan accents.
  • No Fox clone scorebug language that breaks the NAD house identity.
  • No thick SaaS chrome everywhere.
  • No equal-weight cards that flatten hierarchy.
  • No disconnected mini-products; the ticker is the DNA sample for the whole shell.
